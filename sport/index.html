Cable Flyes
Easy Chest 1 min

- Strong stretch: Open chest wide; 2s pause at the bottom

- Mind-Muscle: Squeeze your chest, not your hands, at the top

Incline Bench Press
Hard Chest Drop Set 3 min

- Incline bench at setting 2

- Put a weight plate under the bench

- Scapular Retraction: Keep shoulder blades pinned to the bench

- Leg Drive: Push feet into floor to stabilize heavy load

Weighted Dips
Hard Chest Drop Set 3 min

- Chest Focus: Lean forward and flare elbows slightly

- Safe ROM: Go down until shoulders are slightly below elbows

Skullcrushers
Triceps Medium Drop Set 2 min

- Safe ROM: Lower DBs to the sides of your ears (behind the head)

- Neutral Grip: Palms facing each other to save wrists and elbows

- Fixed Elbows: Keep elbows tucked

Superset
A Lateral Raises
Shoulders Easy 1 min

- Lead with elbows: Hands stay lower than elbows at all times

- Control: Slow negative; don't use momentum to swing the weights

B Incline Rear Delt Fly
Shoulders Easy 1 min

- Incline bench at setting 2

- Keep pinky finger as highest point of the hand

Incline Bench Abs
Abs Medium 2 min

- Stretch: Focus on the stretch at the bottom

TUESDAY
Pull
Back Biceps Forearms Abs
Specific
Warm-up Easy 3 min

- Dead Hangs (2 sets of 45s)

Weighted Pull-ups
Back Hard Drop Set 3 min

- Neutral grip to protect shoulders and maximize load

- Full range: Dead hang at bottom, chin high above bar

- 3 sets of 7-10 reps (Increase weight when 10 reps are clean)

Single-Arm Machine Row
Back Hard Drop Set 3 min

- Symmetry First: Always start with the left arm and match reps with the right

- Chest Glued: No torso rotation; keep sternum against the pad

- Hook Grip: Hands are just hooks; drive the elbow toward the hip

- Stability: Grip the machine frame with your free hand

Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown
Back Medium Drop Set 2 min

- Lats Focus: Pull with elbows; avoid leaning back excessively

Superset
A Straight Arm Pulldown
Back Easy 1 min

- Flat Back: Keep abs tight; do not arch or "stick your butt out"

- Fixed Elbows: Arms slightly bent but locked; pull with the lats

B Bayesian Curl
Biceps Easy 1 min

- Constant Tension: Step forward from pulley; full stretch behind

Hammer Curl
Forearm Easy Drop Set 1 min

- Crush Grip: Squeeze handles to maximize forearm activation

Hanging Knee Raises
Abs Medium 2 min

- Pelvic Tilt: Curl your pelvis toward your chest to engage the lower abs

- No Swing: Control the descent to keep tension on the lower abs
